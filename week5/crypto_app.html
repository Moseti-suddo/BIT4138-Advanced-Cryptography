Cipher Reference
Caesar Cipher Classical
Shifts each letter by a fixed number. Simple substitution cipher with a key space of only 26. Vulnerable to brute force.
Vigenère Cipher Classical
Uses a keyword to apply multiple Caesar shifts. Polyalphabetic — harder to crack but still vulnerable to Kasiski analysis.
AES-256 Modern
Advanced Encryption Standard. Industry-grade symmetric block cipher. Used in banking, government, and secure communications.
